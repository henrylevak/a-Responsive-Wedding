Henry was also on a new adventure in civilian life just being honorably discharged from the United States Marine Corps. According to him, he was immediately smitten by me and made it his personal mission to ask me out. He didn’t exactly have the guts to do it, so he convinced his coworkers to invite me to a concert with him and a few others that weekend. We all went to that concert and had an amazing time!
